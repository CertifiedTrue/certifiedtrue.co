What is CertifiedTrue™?

We’ve built a platform
to authenticate your data.

Enabling businesses to easily capture, store and authenticate digital data and its provenance.

CertifiedTrue™ is a Software-as-a-Service providing a secure method to immutably bind time, location and ownership of data at its origin, simplify the storage and authentication of that data and enable a secure chain of custody. Our proprietary process uses a hybrid of state-of-the-art encryption and blockchain technology. We answer the who? when? and where? of your data - we leave the why? up to you!

CertifiedPhoto™, the first of our suite of SaaS applications, provides a secure process that captures, stores and authenticates a digital photo as well as CERTifying when and where the photo was taken and who took it.

Why CertifiedTrue™?

Competitive Advantage

CertifiedTrue™ is an actual business with cutting edge blockchain, file storage and mobile technology that solves a real problem for multiple industries and has a reliable business model.

Cutting Edge Technology

Our proprietary blockchain network, CERTnet, is built for scale, speed and reliability. More than merely providing proof of existence, the CertifiedTrue™ process creates an unbreakable chain of custody from the moment and place of file creation, immune to tampering or geospoofing

Strongly Incentivized Network

Our network of Validators, who CERTify each transaction on CERTnet, will have the ability to earn substantial fees without the specialized equipment or computational power required by most current consensus algorithms.

A Supporting Business Model

Distributed Ledger Technology is a critical component of CertifiedTrue's SaaS solution. In turn, the CERTnet blockchain will be strongly supported by our business that solves a genuine problem that is costing multiple industries billions of dollars a year.

Stephen Graves

CEO & Founder

Stephen Graves is an innovation, strategy and change executive with 20+ years of executive management and hands on experience in technology, consulting and financial trading.

Most recently, Mr. Graves served as Director of Global Business Operations at CA Technologies, where he managed operational improvements across Business Unit & Operations teams & led multiple C-Suite programs & strategic initiatives. including major transformations in New Product Incubation and creation of the CPO Innovation Forum, a bi-monthly pitch event. As a SME in lean startup methodology, Mr. Graves, enjoyed mentoring numerous intrapreneur teams in refining their business model, pitching and getting funded.

Prior to that, Mr. Graves was a Managing Consultant at Olorin Consulting, where he provided strategic advisory, management consulting and implementation services across a wide range of clients, from startups and small cap businesses, non-profits and government agencies.

His prior business career included founding UNeCom, a digital certificate authority and security startup, working as Associate Staff Analyst for a NYC city agency, and founding Bio Nutrition Research Labs, for the research, manufacturing and sales of nutraceuticals. Mr. Graves was also an NFA registered Principle as a Commodities Trading Advisor and Commodities Pool Operator at BIG Asset Management and hedge fund manager at BIG Tiger Management as well as FOREX trader at Financial Services Investment Group and at GoldStock International.

Mr. Graves is active in multiple non-profit and NGO endeavors. He previously chaired the International Business Advisory Board of the Greater New York Chamber of Commerce and served as Investment Advisor to the Pakistan Board of Investment. Prior to this he was an advisor to former Pakistan Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto and to former Kashmir Prime Minister Barrister Sultan Mehmood Chaudhry.

Mr. Graves has a Masters in Business Administration from Edinburgh Business School, Heriot Watt University and has taken post-graduate studies in National Security at American Military University. He holds professional certifications as a Project Management Professional (PMP), Professional Scrum Master, Certified SAFe Agilist and ITIL.

As an Eagle Scout himself, he has served in volunteer positions for the Greater New York Council of the Boy Scouts of America. He has also served as a volunteer for Prison Fellowship International and as Choir Member for Times Square Church.

Ron da Silva

CIO & Co-Founder

Ron da Silva is an executive leader, international board member and internet technology expert. He brings extensive experience leading network architecture & engineering, internet backbones, broadband networks, cloud infrastructure, and corporate and internet governance. Over twenty (20+) years of proven business experience, culminating in senior executive leadership. NACD certified Board Governance Fellow.

Ron was previously the Group Vice President of Network Engineering, Architecture & Technology for Time Warner Cable (acquired by Charter Communications). At TWC he drove major technology initiatives and was responsible for all networking from customer premise to internet peering, access networks, routing, switching, optical and clould infrastructures. During his tenure with the company, Ron led network convergence, constructed a national backbone, oversaw the implementation of IPv6, helped define and implement DOCSIS 3.0/3.1 and many other major network programs. Ron held prior architecture, engineering and operations roles with America Online and Sprint. He additionally served in technical and planning roles for Bauer Compressors in Norfolk, Va. while completing his Bachelor of Science in applied mathematics and English at Old Dominion University.

Ron is well known in the service provider and cable industries and is very active in various industry organizations. He previously served as Chairman of the Advisory Council for American Registry of Internet Numbers (ARIN), co-chair of the L2TPEXT working group in the Internet Engineering Task Force (IETF), member of the Number Resource Organization’s Number Council (aka Address Serving Organization Address Council), is a member of the SCTE, IETF, ISOC, past speaker and participant at NANOG, CableLabs, NCTA. Ron has experience volunteering on the Board of Directors of various organizations.
